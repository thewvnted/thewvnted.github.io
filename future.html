My Future Aspirations

Dreams, goals, and the path I envision for my future

Career Goals

Short-term (1-2 years)

Complete my degree with honors and secure an internship at a leading tech company. Develop proficiency in at least two new programming languages.

Medium-term (3-5 years)

Work as a software engineer at an innovative company, contributing to meaningful projects. Pursue a master's degree part-time while gaining industry experience.

Long-term (5-10 years)

Advance to a senior developer or team lead position. Potentially start my own tech startup or become a technical architect for large-scale projects.
